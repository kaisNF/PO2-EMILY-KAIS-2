About us

In Auxiliary. We provide families with courses that provide extra support for their children. As we provide an inclusive environment where students can learn and understand with fellow peers, we aim to celebrate their achievements together! Adults can additionally sit through the first few classes for a peace of mind when it comes to their children in this day center. Not only that, but we also provide classrooms, music studios, meeting rooms, and playgrounds to book by the hour for your personal and professional life to be conducted in complete peace and privacy.
