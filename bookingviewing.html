MUSIC STUDIO

$27/hr

Description

We provide studios available to eager musicians looking for a place to record their music. Bookings at Auxiliary cover a wide range of necessities to promote individual growth and foster young talent. The music studio contains a wide range of musical instruments, along with books that contain sheet music containing a variety of genres. Stay connected with other fellow musicians around the block as well! At Auxiliary we want to share talent with the rest of the community, and so we provide open rooms specifically for collaboration with other fellow musicians. Bookings go by the hour.
